Privacy Policy – In-App Version

LeaveANote – Your privacy matters to us

1. We don’t collect personal data

  • No name, email, or contact info is required.
  • You don’t need to create an account to use the app.

2. Location is used only to place notes

  • We use your location to anchor notes in the right spot.
  • We don’t track your movements or store your location history.
  • Your location is never shared with others.

3. Camera access is only for AR

  • The camera helps detect surfaces so you can place notes in augmented reality.
  • We don’t record or save any camera footage.

4. Notes and audio are synced via Firebase

  • Your notes are stored securely so you can access them across devices.
  • Each note includes only text, position, timestamp, and optional audio.
  • Audio recordings are stored securely and linked only to your notes.
  • We don’t analyze, share, or use your recordings for any other purpose.

5. No ads, no tracking

  • We don’t use advertising systems or share your data with third parties.
  • We don’t track you or use your data for marketing.

6. You’re in control

  • You can delete your notes and recordings anytime.
  • Want to remove all your data? Just email us at postitleaveanote@gmail.com

Thanks for trusting us! – The LeaveANote Team
